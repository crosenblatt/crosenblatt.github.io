I am a computer science and math major at Purdue seeking a Summer internship for 2019.
I am especially interested in Android and iOS mobile app development.
See below for more info on my projects, and scroll all the way down to check out my github or send me an email.

Idle Clicker

My first significant foray into iOS development, I created this game during Summer 2018. It is a clicker game featuring resource management, and a leveling system. It also has basic Firebase support, segues to multiple screens, and persistant locally saved data.

Wizard Duel

At my first hackathon, my team worked together to learn Android development and create this app. It is a turn-based combat game with resource management mechanics and custom gestures. We struggled at the beginning to make even simple things work, but at the end of the hackathon we won Best Android App. My contribution included much of the backend for the app including writing the code for the gameplay loop and creating most of the actions.

Boilerfaves

This Android app was created with Purdue's chapter of ACM SIGAPP. The idea is that a user sets their favorite foods (their "faves") and then gets notified when those foods are in a Purdue dining court. My contribution included much of the front end for the page where a user sets notification times, as well as implementing a filtering feature for the main screen. It is available on Google Play here.
